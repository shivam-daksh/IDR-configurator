Overview

IDR is an acronym for "Indian Defence Reforms". In May 2019, we established a presence on Instagram under the same name, sharing daily defence updates encompassing the Indian Army, Navy, and Airforce.

Our content resonated strongly with the public, amassing over 25,000 dedicated followers — whom we affectionately call IDR'ians — by August 2021 across various media platforms.

This overwhelming support inspired us to evolve further. On 13 August 2021, we officially launched our company: IDR Research and Development Pvt. Ltd.

What We Do Under IDR?

Team IDR Research and Development Pvt. Ltd. is focused on pioneering NextGen drone technologies to reshape the "Made in India" vision and contribute robustly to a self-reliant Indian drone ecosystem.

Our operations span across unique verticals that diverge from conventional industry players. Our core vision is to deliver innovative, cost-efficient, indigenously developed drones and supporting technologies.

Beyond drone tech, we offer comprehensive services in:

  • Drone Technology Development
  • 3D Modelling and Prototyping
  • Product Design
  • Research & Development

We provide end-to-end solutions — from conceptual design to the final prototype. We are currently collaborating with the Indian Army in prototyping projects and are eager to expand further.

So, give us work, hold our hands, and we promise — we will never let you down.

Background

Founded out of a sense of purpose and patriotism, IDR Research and Development Pvt. Ltd. has its roots in a grassroots social media initiative. The transition from a digital platform to an incorporated entity signifies our commitment to contribute to national growth through advanced technological development in the defence sector.

Driven by a young, ambitious team with a clear mission — to build a strong, sovereign, and self-sufficient India — our journey is just beginning. With innovation, integrity, and impact at our core, we strive to make India proud on the global front of drone technology and beyond.
